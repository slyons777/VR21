Let's add a recipe for some delicious chocolate chip cookies

We're going to borrow a nice picture from General Mills

Here is what the recipe calls for

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white, oat, or spelt flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 cup sugar, unrefined if desired
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar or coconut sugar
  • 1/3 cup chocolate chips
  • 2 tbsp milk of choice, plus more if needed(Almond Milk, Coconut Milk, Oat Milk)
  • 2 tbsp oil or melted vegan butter
  • 1/4 tsp pure vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Combine all dry ingredients in a bowl, then stir in wet to form a dough.
  2. If needed, add 1-2 tbsp extra milk of choice
  3. Form into one big ball, then either refrigerate at least 2 hours or freeze until the dough is cold
  4. Once dough is chilled, preheat oven to 325 F
  5. Form dough balls, and place on a greased baking tray, leaving enough room between cookies for them to spread
  6. Bake 11 minutes on the center rack
  7. Let them cool on the baking tray 10 minutes before touching
